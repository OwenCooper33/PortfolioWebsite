About

During my childhood in rural Maine, my deep fascination for animals and nature ignited a profound curiosity within me. This innate interest naturally propelled me towards the captivating world of wildlife and nature photography. Seeking a closer connection with the animal kingdom, I embarked on a journey through the lens of my camera, capturing the beauty and essence of the creatures that surrounded me. This passion for photography became a meaningful way for me to not only observe but also to preserve and share the wonders of the natural world.
